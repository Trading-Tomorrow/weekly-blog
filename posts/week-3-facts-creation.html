Week 2

📈 Shaping the Foundation of Our Expert System

2 min read · by Team

During our third week, we moved forward with a more concrete approach to our work. We distributed tasks among the team to start building facts related to specific topics in the coffee domain, laying the groundwork for the knowledge base that will fuel our expert system.

At the same time, we began preparing for the next round of meetings with domain experts. These sessions will allow us to refine our understanding further and ensure that our system captures the nuances of the trading environment as accurately as possible.

Looking ahead, we plan to focus on analyzing and understanding the correlations between the facts we have gathered. This will be a crucial step in shaping the reasoning capabilities of our system, bringing us closer to a tool that reflects the real dynamics of commodity trading.
