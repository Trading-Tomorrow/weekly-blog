Week 3

Team Expansion and Dataset Exploration

2 min read · by Team

In the third week, two new members joined the project team. As part of the onboarding process, we presented the overall concept of the project, including its objectives, architecture, and the main tasks involved in its development. This presentation helped ensure that all team members have a clear understanding of the project's goals and how the different components will interact.
After integrating the new members, we began exploring datasets related to the language component of the system. Since the project includes the development of a language model capable of analysing textual descriptions of accidents and generating structured reports, it is necessary to gather relevant textual data.
