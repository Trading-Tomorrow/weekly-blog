Week 2

Workshop Competition & Robotics Setup

2 min read · by Team

During the second week of our project, we worked on two main fronts: defining the competitive workshop dynamics in multi-agent systems and setting up our robotics workspaces.

Our key achievements and decisions this week include:

  • Workshop Competition (Multi-Agents): Explored competition between auto repair workshops to determine which one offers the best pricing and service for vehicle repairs. Conceived the idea of a competition trail/tracking system to monitor agent bids and histories.
  • Robotics Environment Setup: Spent a lot of time resolving dependencies, installing required software packages, and troubleshooting programs in the robotics course. We successfully compiled and stabilized all required configurations and are now fully ready to work!
