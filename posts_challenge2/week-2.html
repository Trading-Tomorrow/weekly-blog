Week 1

Building the dataset

2 min read · by Team

My first week of the master’s program was mostly about getting to know the courses and understanding what we will be doing this semester. Each professor gave an introduction to their class, explained how we will be evaluated, and what topics we will study. We also had to choose our projects for the different courses. We looked at the available options, talked with classmates, and picked the topics we want to work on during the semester. Overall, it was a calm but important week. I learned what to expect, met new people, and started preparing for the work ahead.
